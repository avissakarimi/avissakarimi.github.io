Hi, I'm Saba

I’m a third-year Ph.D. student in Materials Science and Engineering at Georgia Tech, focusing on integrating data-driven approaches into materials discovery and design. My research combines machine learning, computational modeling, and experimental techniques to accelerate the development of new materials for energy and environmental applications. I’m driven by a passion for solving challenging scientific problems and making a positive impact on the world. Outside the lab, I enjoy mentoring, creating inclusive research environments, and empowering future scientists and engineers. I also love exploring new recipes, traveling, and spending time with my loved ones.
